About This Page

This website introduces three art styles that I think are interesting: Impressionism, Surrealism, and Pop Art.

Three Art Styles

  • Impressionism uses light colors and soft brush strokes.
  • Surrealism shows dream-like and strange ideas.
  • Pop Art uses bright colors and everyday objects.
